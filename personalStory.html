Childhood

I was an outgoing child, playing with the neighbourhood children almost every day during the summer,
up until my early teenage years, when the xbox 360 came into my life, at this point I became an indoor child,

Teenage Years

Unfortunately during my teenage years, my parents began to split, begining with arguments and eventually my father moving out,
followed by divorce procedings, however the divorce was cancelled following a suspected heart attack from my father.
These happening affected me but not to the fullest extent that I was aware of until my adulthood

During my school education, I came to the conclusion that I wished to join the RAF as a Weapons System Operator (crewman).
Unfortunately I did not get the grades in physics required to serve this role. Seeking alternatives into military service, I attended
college and completed a Uniformed Public Service level 3 Diploma in the hopes of joining the army as a Weapons System Engineer.
Alas, these aspirations will also not come to fluision, first I was declined due to deppression being on my medical records,
this needed to be sorted and have no relaps for two years, I was on my way and proud of myself, until I suffered a back injury,
slipping a disc in my lower back. I then learned that once someone suffers a back injury, the military will not accept them.

After changing security jobs several times, I eventually had an accident and was hit off my motorcycle in November of 2018.

education at Bath Spa univerity in 2020 starting a combined degree of Education and History. After getting to the end of my

the general public.

After scoring 99 percentile on my Perseptual reasoning during my education psychologist meeting, I promptly changed my degree to Creative Computing (Gaming)
