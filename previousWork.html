Higher Education Journey

Ryan has been in education a long time, having finished college in 2012, he went to work, and in 2019 returned to college and is now in his third year of higher education at Bath Spa university.

Having originally attending university studying a combined degree of History and Education; although he was getting through this degree, he was managing to get PASS's and only just a getting MERIT at times.

When Ryan was sent to a session for help with study, he was informed that his issues could be related to underlying mental disabilities, he was therefore sent to do some tests, followed by an education psychologist meeting; here he discovered he has Dyslexia, Dyspraxia, and some symptoms of ADHD. This news explained a lot for Ryan, he was also informed he has tallents that are not normal, which he always thought were part of everybodies natural brain functionality.

With this newly aquired knowledge, Ryan saught after a new degree and future, and immdeiately applied for a degree in Creative Computing, gaming pathway.
